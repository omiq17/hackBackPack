Little About Joyoddhoney

JOYODDHONEY is a non-political Cultural organization of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET). It was Established on February 16, 2000 by some creative and innovative students of that time. With the goal of creating a free cultural environment inside CUET and exposing patriotism by different cultural activities, Joyoddhoney has been organizing and performing so many cultural programs for last fifteen years both inside and outside CUET. It is a official facebook group for Joyoddhoney members and well wishers.
Joyoddhoney has been organizing and performing so many cultural programs for last fourteenth years both inside and outside CUET. It is organized a annual program named "JOLRONG" in CUET. It is also organized a three day long national program named "Inter University Cultural Festival(IUCF)".

Dance

Song

A song is a single work of music that is typically intended to be sung by the human voice with distinct and fixed pitches and patterns using sound and silence and a variety of forms that often include the repetition of sections.

Mime

Mime is a person who uses mime as a theatrical medium or as a performance art, involving miming, or the acting out a story through body motions, without use of speech.
